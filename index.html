Amidst the largest property sale in Brussels' history, a former European Commission building is being kept operational by a collective presence at work. As two reporters wander its corridors in search of a manager, routines and roles emerge. Hors Service explores how institutional spaces are performed, sustained, and quietly reshaped by those who occupy them.
